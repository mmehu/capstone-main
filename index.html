About Us

The National Black Food and Justice Alliance (NBFJA) appears to be an incredible coalition dedicated to empowering and supporting Black communities through a focus on food sovereignty, self-determination, and land rights. This commitment to fostering Black leadership, advocating for Black communities, and striving for Black self-determination is immensely important. The emphasis on approaching food sovereignty, land rights, and self-determining food economies through the lens of healing, organizing, and resistance against anti-Blackness shows a comprehensive and holistic approach to addressing the systemic issues that have historically affected Black communities. This work not only aims to create just and sustainable communities but also serves as a platform for amplifying Black narratives, ensuring visibility for Black-led initiatives, and building capacities within food systems and land rights work. Supporting initiatives like the NBFJA is crucial for creating a more equitable society and ensuring that historically marginalized communities have the tools, resources, and agency to shape their own futures.
